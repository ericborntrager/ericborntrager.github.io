A restaurant themed rhythm game with unique controls and fun music!

Programming Contributions

  • Overall audio implementation (Music and SFX)
  • Utilized FMOD for adaptive audio
    • Each instrument in a song (i.e. guitar, bass, drums) could be affected separately
      • i.e. player error may trigger audio effects such as pitch distortion on the guitar or another instrument
      • Instruments could be muted/unmuted individually
    • Allowed for rhythmically accurate “beat callbacks”
      • i.e. unity events could be triggered at the start of a measure or a specific measure/beat in the song
